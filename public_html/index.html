Terms & Concerns

Please read this page before contacting me for an order. Thank you!

Once you have contacted me, I will respond within 24-48 hours.

DETAILS: Please include the date you would like to receive your order, what you would like to order, and quantity (if applicable). Think about flavors, frosting, fillings, toppings, decoration, theme, and any special requests or food allergies. If you aren't sure of these details, no worries-- we'll figure them out together.

DELIVERY: As of now I am only delivering locally, in Ahmedabad. Orders can be delivered to a halfway point, or picked up.

PAYMENTS: Payments under ₹500 will be collected upon delivery or pick-up. Payments over ₹500 require a 50% non-refundable deposit at least a week in advance, to cover the cost of ingredients and materials in the case of a cancellation. The remainder of the total can be paid at the time of delivery or pick-up, or the total amount can be paid for in advance.

CAKES: All orders should be made at least 1-week in advance.Some exceptions can be made.
